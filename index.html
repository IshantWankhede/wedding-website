The nearest railway station is Apta on the Panvel–Roha line. From Apta station, the venue is just across the road or a very short rickshaw ride away.

By Road

Elysium Estate is easily accessible by road from Mumbai, Navi Mumbai and Pune. Please allow extra time for traffic, especially on weekend or evening routes.
